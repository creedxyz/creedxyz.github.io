Creed

Creed has dissolved.

Creed was a security collective specializing in smart contract audits, risk assessments, and penetration testing for web3 teams.

After three years of operation, we've decided to wind down. All existing client commitments have been transitioned. Our members continue to work independently in web3 and application security.
